A Secure Web Wallet Built on TrustedCoin

Open Wallet Open Wallet Create a new wallet

You are still in control of your coins.

TrustedCoin wallet secures your funds with a 2-of-3 multisig. You hold two keys, so we can't move your funds without your permission.

We delay transactions by 24 hours.

Any time someone spends funds from your account, we send you an email and SMS with a link to cancel the transaction within 24 hours.

Your coins are safe even if your computer is not.

You can use this wallet without even entering two of your private keys on the same computer.

That way, even if you have a keylogger running on your machine, no one can steal you coins without giving you 24 hours to reverse.

This wallet demonstrates a reference implementation of a secure wallet built using TrustedCoin APIs. See GitHub repository: https://github.com/jseims/trustedcoin-web-wallet
